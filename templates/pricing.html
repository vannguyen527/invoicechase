Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a monthly subscription?

No — $49 is a one-time payment for lifetime access. No recurring charges, ever.

How does the reminder system work?

You set your payment terms and InvoiceChase automatically sends escalating reminders (friendly → firm → final) via email and SMS if you upgrade. Each reminder is customizable.

What happens when I hit my invoice limit on the free plan?

You'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro. Your existing invoices remain accessible — they just won't receive new reminders until you upgrade.

Can I import my existing invoices?

Coming soon — bulk CSV import is on the roadmap. For now, manually adding invoices takes about 30 seconds each.

What if a client refuses to pay?

InvoiceChase automates the follow-up sequence, not debt collection. It helps you stay professional and persistent — which handles 90% of late payers.
