Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use InvoiceChase:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and password (hashed)
  • Invoice data: Client names, client email addresses, invoice amounts, due dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card or payment details
  • Email metadata: Reminder emails sent on your behalf

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send automated invoice reminders to your clients at the intervals you configure
  • Display your invoice dashboard
  • Process payments via Stripe
  • Improve our Service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Email service provider: For sending reminder emails on your behalf
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a SQLite database hosted on Render's infrastructure. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 and cannot be recovered. While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Client Data

Your clients' email addresses and invoice information are used solely to send reminders on your behalf. We do not use your clients' information for our own marketing or any other purpose.

6. Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your logged-in state. These are essential for the Service to function and are not used for tracking.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@invoicechase.onrender.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or by posting a notice on the Service.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@invoicechase.onrender.com.
